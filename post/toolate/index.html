Too Late Beyond Vietnam

February 8, 2021

  • quotes

We are now faced with the fact that tomorrow is today. We are confronted with fierce urgency of now. In this unfolding conundrum of like and history there is a such thing as being too late. Procrastination is still the thief of time. Life often leaves us standing bare, naked and dejected with a lost opportunity. The tide in the affairs of men does not remain at the flood; it ebbs. We may cry out desperately for time to pause in her passage, but time is deaf to every plea and rush on. Over the bleached bones and jumbuled residue of numerous civilizations are written the pathetic word Too Late

Martin Luther King Jr.
